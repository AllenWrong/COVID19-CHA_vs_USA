Airline adjusts to bring Chinese home from Afghanistan
2021-07-08 16:15
About  Chinese people who had been stranded in Afghanistan returned to China on a Xiamen Airlines flight on Friday, landing in Wuhan of Hubei province. Provided to chinadaily.com.cn    Strict epidemic prevention measures were taken when  Chinese people who had been stranded in Afghanistan returned to China on a Xiamen Airlines flight on Friday, the carrier said. Flight MF took off from Kabul, Afghanistan, and landed in Wuhan, Hubei province. Twentytwo passengers were confirmed infected with COVID, with  asymptomatic carriers, the Hubei Health Commission said on Wednesday. According to the company, each passenger received a serum test once and nucleic acid test twice. One of the nucleic acid test had to be completed within  hours of boarding.
