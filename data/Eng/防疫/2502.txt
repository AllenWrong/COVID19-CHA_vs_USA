China busts over , foreign nationals for illegal border crossing
2020-11-13 10:26
BEIJING  The Chinese National Immigration Administration said Thursday that more than , foreign nationals have been investigated for illegally crossing national borders to enter China this year.Border authorities nationwide have handled over  illegal bordercrossing cases since the beginning of the year, said Yin Chengji, deputy head of the administration, at a press conference.The crackdown on illegal border crossings has effectively prevented inbound cases of COVID through illegal entry channels, Yin added.Since the outbreak of the epidemic,  land points of entry and  border passages across the country have been closed, according to statistics released by the administration.
