Where theres a will, theres a train
2020-06-03 15:59
To ensure the departure of the first block train of epidemic prevention supplies on May , staff members of Chongqing Customs worked all night to ensure that the trains goods were examined and released first. Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn    A train loaded with epidemic prevention materials departed on Sunday from Chongqing and headed to Poznan, a major city in midwestern Poland. The route would pass through Alashankou in the Xinjiang Uygur autonomous region. It was the first rail load of epidemic prevention supplies to be exported via block train by China Railway Express Chongqing, or Yuxinou ChongqingXinjiangEurope International Railway during COVID pandemic. A block train is a freight train that runs as a complete unit from the loading point to the unloading point without intermediate stops.
