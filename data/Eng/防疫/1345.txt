Key meeting emphasizes strong political awareness
2021-01-08 07:21
?        President Xi Jinping. PhotoXinhua    A key Party meeting presided over by Xi Jinping, general secretary of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, on Thursday called for stronger political awareness in pushing for work in all areas and aspects to realize the rejuvenation of the Chinese nation. During the daylong meeting, the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee heard a series of work reports from leading Party members groups of the Standing Committee of the National Peoples Congress, the State Council, the National Committee of the Chinese Peoples Political Consultative Conference, the Supreme Peoples Court and the Supreme Peoples Procuratorate, as well as from the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee. Xi delivered a speech at the meeting. The meetings participants reaffirmed the importance of upholding the authority of the CPC Central Committee and its centralized and unified leadership, and called it a top principle of upholding the Partys leadership and also key to leveraging the countrys institutional advantages, according to a statement released after the meeting. The CPC Central Committees hearing of reports from leading Party members groups of the five State bodies and the Secretariat each year is an important institutional arrangement to uphold the committees authority and its centralized and unified leadership, the statement said. The meetings participants commended the five State bodies work in the past year and acknowledged their contributions in coordinating epidemic prevention and control with social and economic development, the statement said, adding that new and greater progress has been made in all aspects of their work. The Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee was also commended for ensuring the implementation of the decisions and plans of the CPC Central Committee, as well as for improving Party regulations and guiding the work and reform of various organizations. Noting that this year marks the centenary of the Partys founding as well as the first year of the th FiveYear Plan , the meetings participants urged the State bodies to be fully confident of the nations development while remaining vigilant about the risks and challenges on the way ahead.
