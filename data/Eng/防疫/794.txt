Quotable quotes at Zhongguancun Forum
2020-09-21 06:40
The  Zhongguancun Forum kicked off on Thursday in Beijing, gathering global leaders in science and business, along with executives of international organizations, to discuss global innovation and cooperation amid the coronavirus pandemic. Zhong Nanshan, a leading respiratory disease expert and recipient of the Medal of the Republic, and Chen Wei, a recipient of the Peoples Hero national honorary title, shared their insights on using science and technology to fight against COVID. The fourday event with the theme of Meet Challenge with Cooperation and Innovation has attracted many leaders from the science and technology industry to Chinas Silicon Valley, Zhongguancun.
