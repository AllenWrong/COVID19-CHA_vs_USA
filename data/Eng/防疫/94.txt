Wuhan to operate online courses for students amid epidemic outbreak
2020-01-28 21:47
Wuhan, the epicenter of the novel coronavirus outbreak, will begin providing online courses for students in primary, middle and vocational schools on Feb  as a new measure to prevent the further spread of the novel coronavirus pneumonia disease.Spring semester was to begin Feb  for kindergartens, and primary and middle schools open in the capital city of Central Chinas Hubei province. But due to the epidemic, the local education bureau decided to postpone the start of spring semester for all types of schools, including private training institutes.As of Monday at midnight, Hubei had reported , confirmed cases of novel coronavirus, including  deaths.
