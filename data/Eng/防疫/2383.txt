China supports medical supply exports
2020-03-12 16:39
A woman shows a face mask she made in a workshop, during the coronavirus outbreak, in Bangkok, Thailand March , . Picture taken March , . PhotoAgencies    The Chinese government will continue to support foreign trade companies exporting medical supplies such as masks, said the Ministry of Commerce on Thursday. China will expand international and regional cooperation, share experience in epidemic prevention and control, and provide assistance to the countries heavily stricken by the epidemic.
