Bus services resume gradually in Shandong
2020-03-11 15:10
Volunteers disinfect passenger buses in Tengzhou, Shandong province on March , , in preparation for the resumption of bus services in the province. PhotoXinhua    Cities in Shandong province are set to resume intercity and village passenger bus services starting on Wednesday, as part of the provinces effort to restore economic and social development amid the prevention and control of COVID, according to the Shandong Department of Transport. Interprovincial passenger bus services whose departures and arrivals are in lowrisk regions for COVID will also resume gradually. Measures to prevent the spread of the epidemic apply to passenger bus services. For example, vehicles need to be disinfected frequently and longdistance buses must have at least two drivers.
