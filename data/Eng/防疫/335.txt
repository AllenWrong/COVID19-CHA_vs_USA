Transparency can check spread of virus
2020-01-10 00:00
That there has been no panic about the dozens of pneumonia cases in and around the country, primarily in Wuhan, capital of Central Chinas Hubei province, where the number of cases had risen to  on Friday, can be attributed to lessons having been learned from the SARS epidemic about the importance of transparency. The World Health Organization was informed on New Years Eve that a cluster of cases of pneumonia of unknown etiology had been detected in Wuhan.
