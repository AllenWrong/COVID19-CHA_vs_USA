Mutated coronavirus found on patients in Wenzhou?
2020-02-19 15:47
PhotoAgencies    Rumor  percent of the  confirmed cases in Wenzhou were infected with a mutant coronavirus, which is totally different from the virus identified in Wuhan. Fact According to a thesis published by a panel for control of the novel coronavirus epidemic of the Chinese Preventive Medicine Association on Tuesday, fulllength genome sequences of the novel coronavirus samples collected so far are nearly identical, which means there is no obvious mutation. Based on the latest monitoring of the virus, no mutation has been found in any circumstances.
