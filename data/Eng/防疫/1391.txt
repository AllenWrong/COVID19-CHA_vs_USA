Its a wild life out there
2020-02-22 00:00
The new coronavirus has shone a harsh spotlight on the trade in wildlife, an industry some conservation activists reckon needs to be killed rather than tamed.                                             On Feb , the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Standing Committee of the National Peoples Congress stated that it had initiated the revision of the Wildlife Protection Law, planning to add it to its legislative work plan this year, and expedite the revision of laws such as the Animal Epidemic Prevention Law. In addition, the th meeting of the Standing Committee of the th National Peoples Congress, which will be held on Feb , will consider relevant bills on banning illegal wildlife trade, abolishing bad habit of eating wild animals and effectively protecting the health and lives of the people.
