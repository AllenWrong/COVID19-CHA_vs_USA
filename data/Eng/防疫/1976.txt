Discharged patients in Wuhan to undergo day quarantine
2020-02-22 21:45
Inspectors visit a home for a household survey at a residential community in the Jianghan district of Wuhan, Hubei province on Feb , . Photo by Su Fengchinadaily.com.cn    Starting today, novel coronavirus pneumonia patients in Wuhan of Hubei province should be quarantined in designated places for  days after they are cured and discharged from hospitals, the citys epidemic prevention and control authority said on Saturday. It is to ensure the health and safety of the patients and their families. During the quarantine period, they will be under medical observation, which will be for free, the authority said.? After  days, the quarantine will end if they do not show any sign of illness, it said.
