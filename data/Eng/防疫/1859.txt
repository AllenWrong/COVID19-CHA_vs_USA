Experts from Guangxi head for Cambodia
2020-03-23 17:00
A team of seven medical experts from the Guangxi Zhuang autonomous region depart from Nanning on Monday on their way to Phnom Penh, Cambodia, to assist in its fight against the COVID epidemic. Photo by Shi RuipengChina Daily    A team of seven medical experts from the Guangxi Zhuang autonomous region arrived in Phnom Penh, Cambodia on Monday, to assist the Southeast Asian country in its fight against the COVID epidemic Dispatched by Chinas National Health Commission, they come from four hospitals and the Guangxi Center for Disease Control and Prevention.
