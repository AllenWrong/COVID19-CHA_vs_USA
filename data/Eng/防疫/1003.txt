Online learning platform keeps study going for Chinese students amid epidemic
2020-05-14 17:30
BEIJING  China launched a national online education platform to facilitate the continued learning of its  million middle and primary school students who have been confined to their homes amid the novel coronavirus outbreak.Jointly launched by the Ministry of Education MOE and the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology on Feb , the free learning platform covers the  provinciallevel regions on the Chinese mainland.Nearly . billion visits had been paid to the platforms website as of May , said Lyu Yugang with the MOE at a press conference Thursday.The platform has been upgraded to further enrich its resources, which feature  sections including studying courses, epidemic prevention education, moral education, mental health education and classic literature.
