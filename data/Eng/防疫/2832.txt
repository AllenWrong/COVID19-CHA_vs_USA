A quick look at Beijings Dragon Boat Festival travel policies
2020-06-24 15:04
Visitors take photos inside the Palace Museum in Beijing, on May , . PhotoXinhua    Are people allowed to leave Beijing during the threeday holiday? The following three categories of people are strictly prohibited from leaving Beijing  All confirmed cases, suspected cases, their close contacts, asymptomatic carriers, and people with a fever.  Those who have visited Xinfadi market or have had close contact with market personnel since May .  Residents from Beijings medium and highrisk areas. Others should adhere to the principle of not leaving Beijing under unnecessary conditions. Those who must leave Beijing have to provide a negative nucleic acid test report conducted within seven days of departure.
