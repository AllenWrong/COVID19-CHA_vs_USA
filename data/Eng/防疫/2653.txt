Beijings Fengtai displays antivirusthemed flower arrangement
2021-02-03 20:42
Flowers arrangements with the theme of COVID control and prevention are now on display in Beijings Fengtai district.?Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn    Flowers arrangements with the theme of COVID control and prevention are now on display in a gardening center in Beijings southern Fengtai district, which has a long history in the flower industry. Those plants with the special theme not only bring a festive atmosphere to citizens, but also are a reminder to the public that its still necessary to remain cautious of the risks of the COVID epidemic. Many flower businesses in Fengtai are exploring new ways to expand their markets, such as bringing products online. Next step, they will provide flowers with more themes to meet the different needs from the public.
