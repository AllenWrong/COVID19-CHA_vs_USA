Chinas Hubei trains medics to enhance virus prevention, control
2020-03-22 21:15
WUHAN  Authorities on Sunday launched training for medics in central Chinas Hubei Province, center of the novel coronavirus outbreak.The weeklong training will mainly focus on staff from local disease prevention and control centers, hospitals and health centers, according to the National Health Commission and the provincial COVID prevention and control headquarters, which jointly launched the training.Seasoned experts will hold a variety of training sessions to enhance the medics skills to win the battle against the epidemic. About , textbooks will be given out to the medics.
