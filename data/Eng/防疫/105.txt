Wuhan hospital to set up major medical center on epidemics
2020-04-26 15:51
Patients consult medical workers at?Tongji Hospital affiliated with the Tongji Medical College of Huazhong University of Science and Technology, in?Wuhan, Central Chinas Hubei province. Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn    China will set up a national medical center for major public health events at a hospital in Wuhan, Central Chinas Hubei province to lift the countrys capability to handle new infectious diseases and improve the epidemic control mechanism. The center, to be set up at Tongji Hospital affiliated with the Tongji Medical College of Huazhong University of Science and Technology, will carry out research on epidemic control and management models that can help better handle major public health events like the COVID outbreak, according to the hospital.
