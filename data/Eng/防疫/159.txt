WHO wants contact tracing ramped up
2020-07-22 00:00
The World Health Organization on Monday called for steppedup efforts in contact tracing as a major tool to suppress COVID transmission in the face of the coronavirus rapid spread around the world, including among indigenous peoples. WHO DirectorGeneral Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus expressed deep concern about the impact of the novel coronavirus on indigenous peoples in the Americas, the current epicenter of the pandemic.
