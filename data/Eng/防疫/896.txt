Positive COVID test reported in Harbin public activities on hold
2021-09-21 14:32
The city of Harbin, capital of Northeast Chinas Heilongjiang province on Tuesday reported a COVID case that tested positive for the virus, according to the Harbin Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.The case was found in the Second Peoples Hospital of Bayan county in the city and had been transported to Harbin Hospital for Infectious Diseases for medical observation and treatment, the centers said.The citys epidemic prevention and control headquarters on Tuesday released a statement, suggesting suspending trips out of the city if not necessary. Many venues and activities for the public such as educational institutions, entertainment facilities and private clinics are suspending their service.The city also suspended all performances, competitions, forums, exhibitions and other largescale activities until further notice.
