Shenzhen sends  tons of antiepidemic material abroad
2020-03-27 21:31
Four hundred tons of antiepidemic materials and hightech products are departing from Shenzhen to locations overseas from March  to , including Russia, the Czech Republic, Iran and Hungary.On Monday, two chartered flights from Shenzhen Baoan International Airport flew to Prague, Czech Republic and Budapest, Hungary, delivering  tons of epidemic prevention resources  the largest international export cargo in a day since the epidemic began.The airport coordinated with customs and other related departments to ensure green channels for international air cargo shipments.
