Xu Wenbo, head of the National Institute for Viral Disease Control and Prevention under the Chinese Center for Disease Control and Prevention, said the country started vaccination of key groups of individuals in midDecember and the vaccination campaign is progressing in an orderly way. The country will adjust its vaccination plan based on its epidemic situation and progress on vaccine research and development, and supply of vaccines, Xu said at a news conference held by the State Council joint prevention and control mechanism against COVID.
