Overseas is source of latest COVID outbreak in Hebei Expert
2021-01-05 22:24
A staff member disinfects the stairs at a movie theater in Handan, North Chinas Hebei Province, July , .?PhotoXinhua    The source of the latest COVID outbreak in North Chinas Hebei province is overseas, and its highly possible it was imported from Europe, Feng Zijian, deputy director of the Chinese Center for Disease Control and Prevention, said on Tuesday night on a program with China Central Television. Cases in Shijiazhuang and Xingtai have epidemiological connections and are likely to have been infected from the same source, he acknowledged. The number of COVID cases is still increasing in Hebei, which means that the infection has been going on for a while without being detected, he said. Experts from the center and National Health Commission arrived in Shijiazhuang and Xingtai to assist in epidemic intervention. Hebei has seen COVID cases surge since it reported the first confirmed case on Sunday in the latest outbreak. The province reported  new confirmed cases and  new asymptomatic carriers on Tuesday, all locally transmitted, bringing the total number of recently confirmed cases in the province to , according the provincial health commission. The cases are centered in Shijiazhuang and Xingtai cities.
