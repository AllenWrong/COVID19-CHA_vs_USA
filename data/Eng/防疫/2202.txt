Chinese premier urges efforts to consolidate economic recovery
2020-11-17 21:52
BEIJING  Chinese Premier Li Keqiang has called for more effective and sustainable policies to promote steady economic recovery.Li, also a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, made the remarks while presiding over a symposium on the economic situation attended by economists and entrepreneurs on Monday.Noting that the country has made hardwon economic achievements this year despite many difficulties, Li urged regular prevention and control of the COVID epidemic with targeted measures and stressed efforts to consolidate the upward momentum of economic recovery.
