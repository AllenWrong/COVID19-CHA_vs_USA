Chinas Zhejiang to issue movie coupons
2020-07-24 21:09
East Chinas Zhejiang province will issue coupons worth  million yuan about . million to moviegoers from Saturday. PhotoChina News Service    HANGZHOU  East Chinas Zhejiang province will issue coupons worth  million yuan about . million to moviegoers from Saturday to boost the film industry, the provincial film department said on Friday. Moviegoers can get the coupons from a news app and use them when buying movie tickets online. The promotion will last until the end of October. Since Monday, Chinese cinemas in lowrisk areas for COVID have resumed operations with epidemic prevention measures in place. As of Thursday,  cinemas, accounting for nearly half of the total in Zhejiang, have reopened, according to a box office data platform.
