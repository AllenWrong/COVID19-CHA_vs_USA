Financial institutions back nations fight against COVID
2020-02-15 00:00
Multilateral financial institutions showed their support for China amid an outbreak of novel coronavirus pneumonia and voiced their confidence in the resilience of the countrys economy. The New Development Bank, a multilateral development bank established by Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa, said it is ready to make its contribution to the fight by the nation against the epidemic. The NDB is fully committed to supporting China in the time of calamity.
