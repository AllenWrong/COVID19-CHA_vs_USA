COVID prevention rules for transprovincial travel during Spring Festival
2021-01-28 14:32
To cope with the challenging situation of epidemic prevention and control,  provinces, municipalities and autonomous regions released rules for transprovincial travel. People from different risklevel areas have to abide by the following requirements or possess the following materials when traveling to ChongqingFrom lowrisk areas a green health code.From medium or highrisk areas a negative nucleic acid test report conducted within  days or a green health code containing a negative nucleic acid test result have a nucleic acid test locally or a day home isolated observation without these materials.
