Flight will bring students in UK home
2020-04-02 00:00
More Chinese students in the United Kingdom will be able to return to their homeland later this week following an arrangement struck between the Chinese Foreign Ministry and the Civil Aviation Administration of China.Speaking at a daily news briefing, ministry spokeswoman Hua Chunying said the Foreign Ministry is coordinating with the Civil Aviation Administration and other departments to arrange a flight to the UK on Thursday to bring back Chinese students in need.The Communist Party of China Central Committee attaches great importance to and cares deeply about Chinese students abroad, and has taken major measures to protect their safety and health, Hua said.
