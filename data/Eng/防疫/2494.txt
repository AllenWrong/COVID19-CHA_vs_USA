China clears all COVID highrisk areas
2021-10-12 13:32
Airport workers checking the hour nucleic acid testing results of travelers at the HarbinTaiping Airport in Heilongjiang province, on Sept . PhotoXinhua    China has cleared all its COVID highrisk areas, as Northeast Chinas Heilongjiang province downgraded its last two highrisk areas on Monday. Starting at midnight on Monday, two towns in Bayan county of Harbin were downgraded to lowrisk, according to the municipal epidemic prevention and control headquarters. As of Tuesday, only nine mediumrisk areas remain in the country, all in the city of Harbin.
