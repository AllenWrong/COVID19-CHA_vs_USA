Chinese premier to meet press on Thursday
2021-03-09 14:03
The Great Hall of the People in Beijing. PhotoVCG    BEIJING  Premier Li Keqiang will meet the press at the Great Hall of the People after the conclusion of the fourth session of the th National Peoples Congress on Thursday afternoon. Li will take questions from Chinese and foreign reporters via video link in consideration of epidemic prevention and control and public health protection. The press conference will be broadcast live by the China Media Group and on xinhuanet.com.
