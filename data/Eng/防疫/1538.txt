HKbased MD offers classes on virus safety
2020-03-16 00:00
Editors note In this new series, we share stories and experiences showing how expats are dealing with the novel coronavirus pneumonia outbreak.The novel coronavirus outbreak has disrupted work schedules across Asia. To reduce the risk of contracting COVID , many people work from home others have had to cut working hours as the outbreak has slowed business.But for Hong Kongbased doctor Michael Manio, the outbreak has disrupted his weekends. There is no time for rest or play as he chooses to use his weekends making videos on COVID prevention.
