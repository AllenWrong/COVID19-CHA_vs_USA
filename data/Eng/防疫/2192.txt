Chinese table tennis team arrives for Tokyo Olympic Games
2021-07-18 07:15
President of the Chinese Table Tennis Association Liu Guoliang arrives at the Narita airport in Tokyo, Japan, July , . Some members of Chinese Olympic delegation arrived in Tokyo on Saturday. PhotoXinhua    TOKYO  Four squads of the Chinese Olympic delegation, including the Chinese table tennis team, walked out of the Narita International Airport here late on Saturday, after going through strict COVID testing procedures. Liu Guoliang, president of China Table Tennis Association, who has led the alltime leading Chinese paddlers to a clean sweep in the latest three previous Olympic Games, expressed understanding of the antiCOVID measures of the organizers of Tokyo .
