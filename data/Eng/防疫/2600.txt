British PM tells people You must stay at home
2020-03-25 00:00
The United Kingdom has become the latest European country to impose an effective lockdown to contain the spread of COVID, as Prime Minister Boris Johnson announced stringent restrictions on everyday life on Monday and said to people, You must stay at home. British people are only allowed to leave their homes for very limited purposes starting on Monday night such as shopping for daily necessities and medical needs, Johnson announced in a televised address to the nation on Monday evening. The new measures are set to last for at least three weeks and are subject to constant review. He also ordered shops selling nonessential goods to be closed immediately. Libraries, playgrounds, outdoor gyms and places of worship were also ordered to be closed.
