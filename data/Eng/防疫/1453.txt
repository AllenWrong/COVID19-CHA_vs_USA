IMNU students return to campus in tranches
2020-05-15 12:29
Students line up to enter the Shengle campus at Inner Mongolia Normal University on May . Photopeople.cn    Students at Inner Mongolia Normal University in North Chinas Inner Mongolia autonomous region went back to campus in batches from May , according to IMNU officials. According to the plans, a total of  students studying in IMNUs Shengle campus returned to campus on May , while other students in its Saihan campus will start coming back on May . It is estimated that those graduating this summer and all the postgraduates will return to campus at the end of May, or at the start of June.
