COVID tracked in Tianjin, Manzhouli
2020-11-24 14:33
An asymptomatic patient testing positive on Nov  began showing symptoms and was confirmed again on Monday, the Tianjin Health Commission said.During the quarantine period at Haihe Hospital, the patients lungs showed some abnormalities. He was diagnosed as having a mild case of COVID on Monday.In the Inner Mongolia autonomous region, bus services were suspended starting on Tuesday in the city of Manzhouli to keep people from gathering and spreading the coronavirus, the local government said. The city reported two new local cases of COVID on Saturday.Sampling for coronavirus testing of all residents started on Sunday afternoon in Manzhouli. Testing was expected to complete by Tuesday afternoon, one day ahead of schedule, according to the citys epidemic prevention and control headquarters.
