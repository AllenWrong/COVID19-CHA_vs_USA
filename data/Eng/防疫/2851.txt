Chinese families honored for outstanding performance in fighting COVID
2020-05-15 14:05
BEIJING  The AllChina Womens Federation on Friday announced a list of  families in recognition of their outstanding contributions to Chinas fight against COVID.The list not only covers the families of frontline medical staff and police officers, but also includes the families of those who devoted themselves to community epidemic prevention and control, voluntary service and scientific research during the epidemic.For example, Wang Jiantaos family in the southwestern Chongqing Municipality was honored for three generations voluntary participation in community epidemic prevention and control.Since , more than . million Chinese families have been honored for their filial piety, scientific education of children, environmental protection and other reasons.
