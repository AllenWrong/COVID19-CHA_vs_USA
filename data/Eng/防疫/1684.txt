Global solidarity highlighted through CIIE supporting activities
2021-11-08 00:00
Event highlights a wide range of opportunities for enterprises taking part in the experience                                  Despite the big draw of the global exhibitors at the fourth China International Import Expo, the highlevel supporting activities being held during the event are another major part of the exhibition to address the global economic development and share market opportunities. On Sept , the organizer of the fourth CIIE announced the schedule of onsite supporting activities, releasing detailed information about more than  highquality activities during this years exhibition. The supporting activities of the fourth CIIE will play the function of promoting the exhibition and enhancing the CIIEs role in boosting global procurement, investment, cultural exchanges, openness and cooperation, the organizer said. Some of the supporting activities are organized and participated by international organizations such as the United Nations Industrial Development Organization, the United Nations Global Compact, the International Trade Center, the World Intellectual Property Organization and the New Development Bank. Officials and staff members from government bodies such as the government of Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, the Ministry of Commerce, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the General Administration of Customs, the State Administration for Market Regulation, the National Healthcare Security Administration, the National Medical Products Administration and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade also initiated and participated in some of the events. Local governments from Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, Guangdong, Liaoning, Jiangxi, Shanxi, Yunnan, Fujian, the Inner Mongolia autonomous region and the Xinjiang Uygur autonomous region and other regions are involved in the activities. In addition, industrial organizations, exhibitors, buyers, mainstream media, investment and exhibition cooperation units will hold special activities during the exhibition. Regarding the activity types, they include policy interpretations, matchmaking and contract signing, new product release, investment promotion and research release. The policy interpretations and themed supporting activities feature various policy releases, authoritative analyses, indepth interpretations, and trend analyses and judgments, carried out by members of the organizing committee, national ministries and commissions, local governments, industrial organizations, and research institutions to respond to the concerns of exhibitors and buyers, and promote openingup and cooperation. The matchmaking and contract signing activities will be comprised of demand release, supplydemand matchmaking and signing ceremonies organized by trade missions, buyers, exhibitors, partners of buyer invitation and exhibitor recruitment, and support units of the CIIE. The activities are not only consistent with the theme of the CIIE, but are also closely combined with the characteristics of exhibition areas and industries, showcasing the exhibitions mission in promoting policy communication and boosting global trade, the organizer said. According to the organizer, the fourth CIIE will continue to hold trade and investment matchmaking events during the exhibition period via some facetoface and screentoscreen negotiation activities, combining online and offline tools. The supporting activities of the fourth CIIE will be strictly organized in accordance with the principle of epidemic prevention and control of COVID. Therefore, the relevant parties shall strictly implement the requirements of epidemic prevention and control in accordance with their work. In addition, the fourth CIIE will hold more than  special activities at the exhibition booths featuring striking forms and rich contents, from trial distributions, live broadcasts, art performances and product promotion, to new product release and interactive experiences in order to give the visitors and buyers a truly immersive experience. ?
