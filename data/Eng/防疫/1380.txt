Japanese businessman eager to be back at his post
2020-03-17 00:00

And one day later, all the companys seven Japanese managers returned to their posts in Shanghai.My family had second thoughts about my trip to Shanghai at that moment. But I have witnessed the growth of the Chinese branch from scratch, like watching my child growing up.
