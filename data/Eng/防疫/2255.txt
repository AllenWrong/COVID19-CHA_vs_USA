Chinas Zhengzhou downgrades COVID risk level in six areas
2021-08-18 16:01
A medical worker takes a swab sample from a child at a nucleic acid testing site of Wenlian Community in Zhengzhou, capital of Central Chinas Henan province, Aug , . PhotoXinhua    ZHENGZHOU  Zhengzhou, the capital city of Central Chinas Henan province, has lowered the number of areas classified as mediumrisk for COVID transmission on Wednesday. Starting from  pm Wednesday, the COVID risk level of six residential communities was downgraded from medium to low. Authorities have also decided to lift closed management in three communities, the citys epidemic prevention and control headquarters said. On Tuesday, the city reported no new locally transmitted confirmed cases and asymptomatic cases. Since the latest resurgence began on July , the city had reported  locally transmitted confirmed cases and one local asymptomatic case of the novel coronavirus. Zhengzhou has completed the fourth round of allinclusive nucleic acid testing, with over  million people tested.
