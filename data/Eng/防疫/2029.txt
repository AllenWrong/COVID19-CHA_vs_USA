CRCC sends teams to help Nigeria fight virus
2020-04-08 00:00
China Railway Construction Corp, one of the two biggest rail construction contractors by sales revenue in the country, sent the first medical team with experience in fighting COVID to Nigeria on Wednesday to help with the battle against the virus in the country.The team of  members were selected from the groups affiliated hospitals. They are scheduled to work in Nigeria for  days and will provide necessary medical aid, guidance for outbreak prevention and control to CRCCs Chinese and Nigerian employees in the West African country.The Chinese doctors who specialize in such areas as infectious disease prevention, pulmonary and critical care medicine, will also share their practical medical experiences with others in Nigeria.
