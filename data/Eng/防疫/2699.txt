Beijing Marathon canceled due to COVID
2021-11-26 20:33
BEIJING  The Organizing Committee of the Beijing Marathon announced on Friday that the  Beijing Marathon will be canceled due to COVID.The Beijing Marathon has been cancelled for a second consecutive year in order to protect the health and safety of athletes and citizens, as well as control the risk of the possible spread of COVID.The organizing committee will reserve places for all the contestants who have obtained the qualification this year until . The reserved contestants must meet the registration requirements and relevant epidemic prevention regulations for the  Beijing marathon.The organizing committee will refund all the paid contestants. The registration fee will be returned in full within  working days from the date of announcement.
