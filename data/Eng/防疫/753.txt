Remote working prompts rise in practical innovations
2020-04-15 10:24
A science teacher performs an experiment with water via a livestream in Hangzhou, Zhejiang province, last month. Online classes have become increasingly popular since schools were closed to prevent the spread of the novel coronavirus. HU JIANHUANFOR CHINA DAILY    As China continues to battle the novel coronavirus outbreak, large numbers of people are working from home. That will continue for an indefinite period, despite growing optimism that the situation on the Chinese mainland is gradually returning to normal and a growing number of sectors are tentatively returning to work.
