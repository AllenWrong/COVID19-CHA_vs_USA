Chinas Guangdong adds new COVID risk area
2021-06-19 17:40
GUANGZHOU  The city of Dongguan in South Chinas Guangdong province on Saturday added a mediumrisk area for COVID outbreak, according to the citys epidemic prevention and control headquarters.The move comes after a new confirmed COVID case was reported on Friday in a residential quarter in the citys Nancheng subdistrict. The new case is the wife of a COVID patient earlier confirmed in Shenzhen.As of  pm Friday,  close contacts of the new case had been put under medical observation.The city has initiated mass nucleic acid testing covering residents in areas where the case had visited. Restrictions on personnel movement have also been enforced in certain areas.
