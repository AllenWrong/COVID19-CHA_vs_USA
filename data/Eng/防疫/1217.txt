Medical team arrives in Burkina Faso
2020-04-17 12:30
Chinas first medical team aiding Africans COVID protection and control from Tianjin Peoples Hospital arrived Ouagadougou, capital of Burkina Faso, on April . Photo by Liang LiangFor China Daily    Chinas first medical team sent to Burkina Faso in West Africa   experts to help the country protect itself against COVID  arrived in Ouagadougou, the capital, on Thursday. The team, assigned from Tianjin Peoples Hospital, has specialists in intensive care, respiratory diseases, infectious diseases, traditional Chinese medicine, medical imaging, epidemic research, disinfection and medical care.
