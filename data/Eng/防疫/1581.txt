Hebei sees discharge of st dozen patients
2021-01-15 00:00
Hebei province saw the first  coronavirus patients being discharged from the hospital on Thursday afternoon. They are the first in the province to recover from this years outbreak amid a continued uptick in cases.  The recoveries have bolstered the confidence of all healthcare workers, said Wu Shucai, president of the Hebei Chest Hospital in Shijiazhuang, a designated COVID treatment facility. Wu added that continuous efforts will be made to help more patients recover.  Authorities in Hebei reported another  confirmed local COVID cases in the previous  hours on Thursday, raising the total number of cases in the province to .  Seventyfive of the new COVID patients tested positive in the provincial capital Shijiazhuang, and six were from the nearby city of Xingtai, according to the provincial health commission.  The commission also said that  asymptomatic carriers were placed under medical observation.  The province also reported one death in Shijiazhuang on Wednesday, the first from the latest resurgence of COVID in the province, which started on Jan .  Yan Xixin, an epidemiologist at the Second Hospital of Hebei Medical University who heads the expert panel for treating COVID patients in the province, said the yearold woman had serious existing heart conditions. The virus caused multiple organ failure, including the kidneys and lungs, which led to her passing.  The woman, who was from the citys Gaocheng district, had tested positive on Saturday.  Tong Zhaohui, an expert with the National Health Commission who is helping to treat COVID patients in Shijiazhuang, told China Central Television that the average age of the patients is ., and the average age of patients in critical condition is above .  The recent surge in new COVID cases has prompted authorities in Hebei to postpone their annual provincial legislative meetings.  The Standing Committee of the Hebei Provincial Peoples Congress announced that its annual session, which is scheduled on Jan , will be postponed until further notice.  Similar strain detected  Heilongjiang province had reported  confirmed cases and  asymptomatic carriers as of  pm on Thursday, after the first infections were detected on Sunday.  Gene sequencing shows that the virus is very similar to the strain circulating in the recent outbreak in Dalian, Liaoning province, said Guo Yanru, deputy director of the Suihua center for disease control and prevention on Thursday, adding that further epidemiological investigations are underway.  Heilongjiang witnessed soaring infections in rural areasmuch like the rising cluster of infections in Hebei villagesmaking such areas a new focal point in epidemic prevention and control.  Fang Qingwei, deputy director of the Heilongjiang provincial health commission, said there will be an emphasis on migrants in rural regions getting nucleic acid tests. All kinds of gatherings in rural areas, including weddings, funerals and banquets, will not be allowed in order to contain the spread of the virus.  Villagers in the rural areas of Suihua are all in the slow winter season, which gives them more spare time for indoor activities. Poor ventilation will greatly increase the risk of spreading infections, said Gao Fei, an expert with the Heilongjiang provincial center for disease control and prevention.  Neighboring Jilin province also identified two local asymptomatic carriers on Thursday. Both had close contact with asymptomatic carriers in Heilongjiang, according to the provincial health commission.  The commissions epidemiological investigation showed that another five asymptomatic carriers, which were earlier reported from Monday to Wednesday, were all linked by travel on the No K train on Jan .
