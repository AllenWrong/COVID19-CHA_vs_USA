New technologies aid epidemic prevention and control
2020-04-02 14:02
A woman sees a robot doctor in Beijing, April , . PhotoXinhua    New technologies have been widely used for epidemic prevention and control in China. Robot doctor, rapid temperature measurement device, mobile phone or voice control elevator, noncontact food delivery and disinfection robot are some of the important applications of cuttingedging technologies. Since the novel coronavirus pneumonia outbreak, the innovation center in Zhongguancun and hightech companies have launched a slew of new technological products to fight against the virus.
