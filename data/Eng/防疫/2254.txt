Chinas Zhengzhou further steps up epidemic prevention, control
2021-08-06 15:10
A medical worker takes a swab sample for a COVID test at a testing site in Jinshui district of Zhengzhou, Central Chinas Henan province, Aug , .    ZHENGZHOU  The central Chinese city of Zhengzhou, capital of Henan province, further tightened epidemic prevention and control measures after a recent resurgence of COVID infections, according to local authorities on Thursday. The city classified  new areas as mediumrisk for COVID on Thursday, and began its second round of nucleic acid testing at  am Thursday and will complete the sampling process at  pm Friday. According to education department, the citys primary school enrollment has been postponed. Henan reported  locally transmitted confirmed COVID cases and  asymptomatic cases from July  to August  in the latest resurgence of COVID, the provincial health commission said Friday.
