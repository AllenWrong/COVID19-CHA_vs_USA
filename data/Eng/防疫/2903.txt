Photo by Huang Shifengchinadaily.com.cn    The National Health Commission has set up six medical teams with a total of , personnel to assist in the response to the novel coronavirus epidemic in Wuhan, Central Chinas Hubei province on Saturday. Three medical teams from Shanghai, South Chinas Guangdong and the Peoples Liberation Army have arrived in Wuhan, the epicenter of the outbreak. Six reserve medical teams are on standby. Experts in critical care medicine also have been appointed to strengthen clinical guidance for the treatment of patients.
