Christmas may fuel infection fire
2020/11/19 9:14
TAIPEI  Taiwan will require all inbound passengers to submit a negative COVID test result before boarding a plane to the island, the islands epidemic monitoring agency said on Wednesday. Passengers set to visit Taiwan or transit through here, regardless of their identities or reasons for visiting, must present a negativeresult certificate of a nucleic acid test taken within three days of boarding at the time of checking in, the agency said in a statement. The move is one of the new COVID control measures that will be in place from Dec  and last until Feb  next year. Other measures include the requirement of maskwearing at eight types of public venues such as medical institutions, public transport facilities, and schools.
