Expect rise in patients with both flu and COVID, warns top expert
2020/11/29 14:28
A health worker takes a swab sample from a resident for COVID test in Kashgar, Northwest Chinas Xinjiang Uygur autonomous region, Nov , .  With the arrival of the flu season, more patients will be infected with both the influenza and the novel coronavirus in the near future, said a leading respiratory disease expert. We need faster and more efficient testing technologies and facilities to detect the virus C the flu or the COVID, said Zhong Nanshan, a prominent respiratory disease expert, during a health science and technology innovation conference in Shenzhen, Guangdong province, on Friday. There have already been reports of four cases infected with both the influenza A H and H and the COVID viruses, according to Zhong. Wearing masks is still of great importance to fight against both the influenza A and the novel coronavirus, said Zhong.
