ABUJA  The Nigerian police said on Friday they were launching aerial surveillance to search for the schoolgirls who were kidnapped in the countrys northwestern state of Zamfara. A total of  students from the Government Girls Secondary School in Jangebe town of Zamfara were abducted in the early hours of Friday. Calling the incident barbaric and callous, a spokesperson for the police said two surveillance helicopters had been deployed to ensure the success of the ongoing operation to rescue them.
