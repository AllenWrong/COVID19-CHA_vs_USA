Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn An exhibition of cultural relics from the ancient civilization of the Andes in Peru will be launched at Guangdong Museum in Guangzhou, Guangdong province on Friday. The more than  pieces on display come from  Peruvian museums and Guangdong Museum. They include pottery and metal wares, textile items and mummies. The exhibition will run through May .
