Bangladesh fetches COVID preventive medical aids from China
2020/4/20 10:00
A Bangladesh Air Force transport aircraft returns with medical aids from China in Dhaka, Bangladesh. April , .  DHAKA  Bangladesh Air Force BAF has conducted a special ferry flight to fetch home COVID preventive medical aids from China, the Inter Service Public Relations ISPR said here Sunday. ISPR, a department under the defence ministry, said in a statement that the BAF CJ transport aircraft returned to Dhaka from China on Sunday with medical aids including COVID detection kits and personal protective equipment PPE. It said the preventive medical aids consist of surgical masks, N face masks, protective gloves, electronic thermometers, medical safety glasses, PPEs, goggles and testing kits.
