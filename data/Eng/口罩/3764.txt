Photo provided to China Daily The National Centre for the Performing Arts will reopen on June , ending a nearly fourmonth closure because of the coronavirus pandemic. From Tuesday to Sunday,  am to pm, audiences can buy tickets via advance online reservation with their identity cards. A daily limit of  people has been set. The ticket price is  yuan per person. With one identity card, an audience member can book three tickets at most. The body temperature of visitors must be checked before they enter. Other requirements include the wearing of face masks a green QR code on visitors mobile phones, indicating good health and social distancing of  meter. The museums tour guide service has not resumed.
