TOKYO  The Chinese sailing team arrived in Tokyo on Saturday,  days ahead of the Olympic Games. The member team, including  athletes, is Chinas first Olympic contingent to arrive in Japan. They will compete in eight events in the postponed Games, as Chinas secondlargest presence in Olympic sailing competitions after Beijing . The Chinese team, all wearing masks and eye shields, walked out of the Narita airport before taking an official coach to their hotels.
