Summer Palace resumes boat tours
2020/4/18 16:23
Visitors go on a boat tour on the Kunming Lake in Beijings Summer Palace, on April , . Photo by Jiang Dongchinadaily.com.cn Boat tours resumed operation in the Summer Palace in Beijing on Saturday. The service, which was suspended due to the novel coronavirus, restarted with measures such as sanitation, flow control and limiting the number of passengers in place to protect visitors health and safety. Travelers are also required to wear masks on boat, and get realname registration and health QR codes checked when buying tickets.
