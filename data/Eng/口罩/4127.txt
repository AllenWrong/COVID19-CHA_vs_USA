Beijing issues vouchers to boost cultural consumption
2020/5/7 18:14
A staff member of a shop wearing a face mask passes sugarcoated haws to a child next to a statue at the Qianmen street in Beijing, May , .  Beijing has begun issuing evouchers to promote consumption in the cultural sector. The vouchers, issued by the Stateowned Cultural Assets Administration Center, have a total value of about  million yuan  million to be used for the purchase of books, art performances, tourism, education and entertainment programs. Residents of the capital can get coupons through a WeChat public account and receive cash rebates when making payments.
