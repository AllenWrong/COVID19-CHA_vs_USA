Video Saluting lifesaving medics on Chinese Doctors Day
2020/8/19 6:45
They are fathers, sons and husbands, as well as mothers, daughters and wives. But in the face of diseases and viruses, they are the doctors and nurses people rely on to save lives. Their important efforts in safeguarding peoples health are worth celebrating  and today, were marking the third Chinese Doctors Day to honor them. We may not see their faces behind those masks, but they are angels in our hearts. We may not know their names, but we will remember them forever. Today, lets salute all medical workers!
