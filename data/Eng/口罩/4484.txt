CHANGSHA  The customs of Changsha, capital of Central Chinas Hunan province, said it has strengthened inspection of medical supplies for export. Two batches of masks were detained for further handling at the citys airport on Saturday due to incomplete information on quality certificate and unmatched item number with the registration information at the customs. The customs said companies that fail to declare truthfully or meet quality standards will be punished strictly, while lawabiding companies will enjoy convenient customs clearance.
