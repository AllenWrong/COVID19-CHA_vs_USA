US extends tariff exclusions on  categories of medical goods
2021/3/11 13:59
The United States is extending tariff exclusions on about  categories of medical products from China until Sept , , to help the fight against the COVID pandemic, according to an announcement from the US Trade Representatives office on Wednesday. The exclusion list covers a wide range of items from surgical masks and gloves to blood pressure cuff sleeves and Xray tables. Previously, tariffs on those medical products were excluded until the end of March.
