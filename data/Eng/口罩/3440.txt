Ming Tombs in Beijings suburb closes indoor areas amid epidemic
2020/6/21 9:58
Image of Ming Tombs  BEIJING  The Ming Tombs, a major tourist site in Beijings suburb, announced the temporary closure of its indoor spaces from Saturday amid a resurgence of fresh COVID cases over the past week. The outdoor spaces of the tourist site are still open to the public from  am to  pm every day, according to the office administering the site. Visitors must make realname reservations and purchase tickets online. Besides, visitors are also required to wear masks, show health codes and undergo temperature screening before entry.
