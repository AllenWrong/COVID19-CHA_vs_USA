Infected Olympic star breathing very badly
2020/5/11 9:59
Olympic beach volleyball silver medalist Marcio Araujo said on Saturday that he is battling a severe case of COVID from home after being turned away from overwhelmed hospitals in Brazils northeastern city of Fortaleza. Araujo posted a photo of himself on Instagram, wearing a face mask and holding a sign with the words I will recover, God willing. Unfortunately I have been infected with the coronavirus, he said in a brief message accompanying the image. The yearold told of his ordeal in a conversation with Brazilian volleyball website, Web Volei. Im not very well, he said. I came home from the hospital because there are no intensive care beds. Im breathing very badly, hoping to improve. Xinhua  Agencies
