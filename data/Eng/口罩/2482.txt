China warns against travel to Czech Republic
2020/9/11 20:01
Passengers wearing protective masks walk through the train transfer tunnel at Pragues main railway station, as the Czech government bans all passengers without face protection from the public transport to slow the spread of the coronavirus disease COVID, Czech Republic, Sept , .  The Ministry of Culture and Tourism issued a notice on Friday warning Chinese citizens not to travel to the Czech Republic at the moment due to signs of a rapid rebound of COVID in the country.
