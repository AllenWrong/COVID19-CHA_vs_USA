Medical supplies and other donations from Inner Mongolia autonomous region were sent to support the fight against COVID in Mongolia on Saturday. Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn China donated a second batch of antiepidemic materials worth of , yuan , to Mongolia on Saturday. The donation contained medical supplies, including masks, gloves, disinfectant and other items donated by the foreign affairs office of the Inner Mongolia autonomous regions Xilin Gol League and the leagues border management bureau. The supplies will be distributed to three provinces Sukhbaatar, Oriental and Kent in eastern Mongolia as well as to the Mongolian border defense department.
