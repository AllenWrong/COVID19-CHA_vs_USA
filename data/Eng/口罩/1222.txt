CCB staff member sticks to post during holiday period
2021/2/20 16:10
Tan Shuting, a staff member at an outlet of China Construction Bank CCB in Changchu, Northeast Chinas Jilin province, chose to stay put for this years Spring Festival. At  oclock in the morning, the bank outlet officially opened for business. As the lobby manager, Tan not only had to maintain order in the lobby, but also helped solve various business problems for customers. Cash business increased during the Spring Festival. Since before Spring Festival, we have entered the busiest time of the year, said Tan. The bank outlet is a day business branch, and six staff members were on duty every day during the Spring Festival. In order to ensure the safety of staff members, antiepidemic materials such as masks and disinfectant wipes were provided.
