Fujian donating  boxes of medical supplies to the Philippines
2020/4/2 19:34
Quanzhou city in East Chinas Fujian province will donate over  boxes of medical supplies to the Philippines this weekend to help the country fight against the COVID outbreak. Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn Quanzhou city in East Chinas Fujian province will donate over  boxes of medical supplies to the Philippines this weekend to help the country fight against the COVID outbreak. The supplies, worth over  million yuan ,, include breathing machines, face masks, protective suits, goggles, thermometers and traditional Chinese medicines.
