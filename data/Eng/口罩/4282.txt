CRRC Changchun Railway Vehicles, a company based in Changchun, capital of Northeast Chinas Jilin province, on behalf of its parent company CRRC donated , masks to the transportation department of Serbia on April   to assist in the novel coronavirus epidemic fight in the country, according to local reports.
