Cruise ship with immersive show reopens in Wuhan
2020/6/25 11:17
Viewers take photos on the deck of the Zhiyin cruise ship in Wuhan, Hubei province, on June , . The cruise ship, which travels along the Yangtze River and features a multidimensional and immersive play, reopened to public on Wednesday evening. All viewers are required to wear facial masks, keep safe distances, show their green health codes, and submit to body temperature checks. Medical workers can make online reservations with their work permits or professional qualification certificates and watch the show for free.
