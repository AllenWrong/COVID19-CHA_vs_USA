Shanghai holds exhibition on antiepidemic battle
2020/4/9 11:38
.org An exhibition featuring the battle against the novel coronavirus epidemic opened Wednesday in Shanghai. More than  works are on display at the China Art Museum, including photos, paintings and sculptures. The exhibits were selected from more than , works, according to the organizer. Besides the works, a giant mask consisting of more than  metal masks has drawn much attention. Masks signify safety and protection, and I hope the huge mask will bring strength and hope to the people, said creator Ding She. The exhibition will last until May .
