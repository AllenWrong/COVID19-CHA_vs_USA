NANJING  Nanjing, a megacity with a population of more than . million in eastern China, launched the third round of allinclusive nucleic acid testing campaign on Wednesday, according to the municipal COVID prevention and control headquarters. The citywide testing covers all residents, including those who are paying a short visit to the city. Nanjing, capital of East Chinas Jiangsu province, has seen a resurgence of locally transmitted COVID cases since a number of airport workers tested positive for coronavirus on July . The city now has classified four areas as highrisk for COVID and  as mediumrisk areas, according to the municipal COVID prevention and control headquarters. On Tuesday, the citys center for disease control and prevention said the virus strain behind the recent spike in COVID cases has been identified as the highly infectious Delta variant. Officials therefore have urged those queuing up for the test to wear masks, keep at least one meter apart and avoid chitchat.
