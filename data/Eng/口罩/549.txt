Intl beer festival opens in Qingdao
2021/7/19 11:04
 The st Qingdao International Beer Festival, one of the largest beer festivals in China, opened on Friday evening in the coastal city of Qingdao, East Chinas Shandong province. The day carnival has gathered more than , beers from over  countries and regions, according to the tourism commission of the Xihaian West Coast New Area, where the festival is taking place. There will be over  activities covering international exchanges, economic and trade exhibitions, fashion shows, parades and performances during the festival. Nine tents and  cabins have been set up to provide beer and food for visitors, according to the organizer. This year, the beer festival has asked attendees to wear masks and display their health codes when entering the venue to prevent potential COVID outbreaks. Qingdao is home to the Tsingtao Brewery, the base of a timehonored beer brand in China. Its international beer festival is an annual event.
