Hong Kong airport to resume transits from mainland to elsewhere
2020/8/13 14:06
A photo taken through thick glass shows passengers, center, wearing face masks as a precautionary measure against the COVID coronavirus as they receive quarantine tracking wrist bands at Hong Kongs international airport, March , . ANTHONY WALLACE  AFP HONG KONG  Travelers from the Chinese mainland will be able to transit in Hong Kong International Airport to other destinations from Saturday through Oct , but the transit service for the other way around is still suspended, the Airport Authority Hong Kong said Thursday. Hong Kong International Airport has resumed transit services since June . Passengers are required to have boarding passes under the same ticket if their connecting flights are operated by different airlines. Their luggage must be labeled with a tag to the destination. Travelers are also required to complete transfers within  hours.
