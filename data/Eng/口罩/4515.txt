Freight train loaded with m masks departs China for Russia
2020/4/20 16:46
NANJING  A ChinaEurope freight train loaded with  million face masks departed East Chinas Suzhou city on Monday and will arrive in Moscow in  days. In response to a supply agreement signed between China Sinopharm International Corporation and Russia in March, protective gear began to assemble in Suzhou before being transported to Russia on ChinaEurope freight trains. Zhang Fan, general manager in charge of the freight train service with Suzhou Newcity Investment and Development Company, said over  TEUs foot equivalent units of supplies such as surgical beds and syringes are expected to be sent to Europe over the next month. Statistics show that the number of freight trains and containers have both seen an increase of over  percent in Suzhou in March.
