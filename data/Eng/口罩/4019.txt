Medical experts return favor to Zimbabwe
2020/5/12 14:20
Twelve medical experts from Hunan province?depart for Zimbabwe to help it fight the coronavirus pandemic, May , . Photo provided to China Daily A medical team consisting of  experts left Changsha, Hunan province, for Zimbabwe on Monday to help it fight the coronavirus pandemic. The chartered plane also carried medical supplies, such as ventilators, testing instruments and masks that were donated by the Hunan government. African countries, including Zimbabwe, offered help when the coronavirus hit China, and now its time for China to return the favor, provincial authorities who saw the experts off at the Changsha airport said. The team will stay in Harare, Zimbabwes capital, for two weeks to offer treatment suggestions and share prevention experience with local people.
