Countries sign up to historic resolution to fight COVID
2020-05-21 06:58
On Monday, heads of government from around world took part in a virtual World Health Assembly gathering to reach a historic consensus resolution on how to combat COVID.Speaking at Wednesdays regular media briefing, WHO DirectorGeneral Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus outlined the organizations response.I welcome member states commitment to lift all barriers to universal access to vaccines, diagnostics and therapeutics, he said.
