PLA delivers vaccines to Pakistani, Cambodian armies
2021-02-07 17:20
The Chinese Peoples Liberation Army PLA on Sunday delivered a batch of COVID vaccines to the Pakistani military at the latters request and with the approval of the Central Military Commission, Chinas Ministry of National Defense said Sunday. PhotoCCTV    The Chinese Peoples Liberation Army on Sunday delivered a batch of COVID vaccines to the Pakistani and Cambodian military at their request and with the approval of the Central Military Commission, Chinas Ministry of National Defense said Sunday. The Pakistani and Cambodian armies have become the first batch of foreign militaries to receive COVID vaccine aid from the Chinese military, according to a statement issued by the ministry. The Chinese PLA will continue to make contributions to building a global community of health for all, the statement added.
