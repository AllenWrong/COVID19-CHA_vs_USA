Seven years on, Xis vision of civilization inspires hope in world of uncertainties
2021-03-27 14:30
Visitors take a selfie in front of China culture pavilion during the  Carassauga Festival in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada, May , . PhotoXinhua    BEIJING  We need to encourage different civilizations to respect each other and live together in harmony while promoting their exchanges and mutual learning as a bridge of friendship among peoples, a driving force behind human society, and a strong bond for world peace, Chinese President Xi Jinping said in  at the headquarters of the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization in Paris. Seven years have passed, but Xis vision of civilization featuring diversity, equality and inclusiveness in his UNESCO keynote speech is still reverberating in a world that is grappling with the raging COVID pandemic, as well as global political and socioeconomic challenges.
