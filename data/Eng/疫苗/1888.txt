PhotoAgencies    MADRID  Spain will allow people from nonEU countries who have been vaccinated against COVID enter the country from June , Prime Minister Pedro Sanchez said on Friday. The new rule will apply to travellers from the United States, Sanchez pointed out.  Agencies via Xinhua
