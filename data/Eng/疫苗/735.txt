Doctor calls for sciencebased approach for tracing COVID origins
2021-08-31 19:25
DHAKA  Only scientists should study the origins of COVID, the leader of the Bangladesh Medical Association, the largest nationwide professional body of medical doctors in the country, told Xinhua on Tuesday.If we have a clear and transparent idea about its origins or source, it will be an easy task to deal with the virus, said Mustafa Jalal Mohiuddin, the associations president.Mohiuddin, a former Member of the Bangladesh Parliament, hailed China for supporting his country with COVID vaccines.Definitely we applaud Chinese support, he said, adding that if rich countries fail to help their less fortunate counterparts with vaccines, medical equipment and technology, the world cant expect a return to normalcy.Mohiuddin said the Chinese government has taken the right approach to controlling the virus.
