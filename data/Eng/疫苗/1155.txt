Shenzhen launches pilot autonomous taxi service
2021-07-22 09:20
SHENZHEN  The southern Chinese metropolis of Shenzhen has for the first time launched a pilot autonomous taxi service for the public.Shenzhenbased company DeepRoute said its robotaxi program is available to the public starting Monday, making it the first company to provide this type of service in Shenzhen.The company worked with the Futian district government to deploy  selfdriving taxis in the city.DeepRoute said the vehicles have nearly  pickup and dropoff points, including popular landmarks and COVID vaccination centers, with the service covering over  km of public roads in Shenzhen.The taxis are free of charge and open to people aged over  through an application process on DeepRoutes official WeChat account.
