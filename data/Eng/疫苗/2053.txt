PhotoAgencies    BAKU  Azerbaijans acting health minister Teymur Musayev on Thursday hailed the CoronaVac vaccine made by Chinas biopharmaceutical company Sinovac Biotech as fully safe. Speaking at a press briefing of the operational headquarters for COVID prevention and control, Musayev said that . million people in the country have got their first dose of the Chinesemade vaccine so far, while , people have received both. He said Azerbaijan enrolled the CoronaVac vaccine for mass vaccination, describing it as completely safe. Infection cases among those who received both doses were minor, he said. We embark on a new phase of vaccination against COVID in Azerbaijan from May  that will enable citizens above  to be vaccinated. This means we now have sufficient quantities of the jab, Musayev said. Azerbaijan launched the mass vaccination in January.
