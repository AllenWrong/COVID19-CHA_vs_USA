Bangladesh approves purchase of a batch of COVID vaccines from China
2021-08-12 09:30
A batch of COVID vaccines arrives at Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Aug , . PhotoXinhua    DHAKA  The Bangladeshi government has approved the purchase of a batch of COVID vaccines from China. Bangladeshs Cabinet Committee on Government Purchase CCGP Affairs at a meeting approved the purchase on Wednesday. Shamsul Arefin, a senior Cabinet Division official, told journalists after the meeting that Bangladesh will buy the vaccines from China National Pharmaceutical Group Sinopharm. The Bangladeshi government has recently launched countrywide a mass vaccination drive following the surging COVID positivity rate in parts of Bangladesh since June.
