Nanjing reports COVID case
2021-07-12 13:06
Nanjing, Jiangsu province, reported a confirmed COVID case on Monday on its WeChat account  a close contact of an imported asymptomatic case in neighboring Anhui province.The COVID control office of Nanjings Yuhuatai district received notice from Anhui province about the contact, surnamed Xiao, on Friday and immediately sent him to a quarantine center.Xiao tested negative on Friday but was then found positive on Sunday. He has been transported to a designated hospital for treatment.The man, who had not been vaccinated, traveled to Nanjing for business on Wednesday. Three of his close contacts have also been quarantined for observation.
