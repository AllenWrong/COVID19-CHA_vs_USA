Easing of lockdowns set amid festive runup
2020-11-26 00:00
With the upcoming festive period, some countries are planning to ease COVID lockdowns even as the number of global coronavirus cases continues to grow, according to media reports.  Measures to deal with coronavirus outbreaks remain in place across Europe, but a reduction in daily reported cases in some areas has led countries to revisit their restrictions.  French President Emmanuel Macron announced a threestage easing of the countrys monthlong lockdown, beginning this weekend with the reopening of nonessential businesses such as hairdressers and clothes shops, which will need to observe strict distancing rules and close at  pm, according to British newspaper The Guardian.
