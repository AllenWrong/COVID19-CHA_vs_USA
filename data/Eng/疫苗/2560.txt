Donated doses lift spirits in Sri Lanka amid second wave
2021-04-03 00:00
Sri Lankans battling a virulent second wave of the coronavirus pandemic have been buoyed by the arrival of donated supplies of a Chinesedeveloped COVID vaccine. Vaccinations with the shot, from Chinese drugmaker Sinopharm, will begin on Monday, the presidential office said in a statement.
