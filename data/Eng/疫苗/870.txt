All areas in Shanghai to be classified as lowrisk for COVID
2021-08-16 20:02
Teenagers stay for medical observation after the vaccination with their parents at a vaccination point in Shanghai, China, Aug , . PhotoXinhua    The only residential community in Shanghai designated as a COVID mediumrisk region on Aug  following the emergence of a new case living in the neighborhood will be adjusted to a low risk area on Tuesday, according to the citys pandemic prevention and control authorities. A male patient who handles cargo flights for a foreign airline company at Pudong International Airport and lives in the community in Pudongs Chuansha town was confirmed as a COVID case on Aug . No more related cases have been reported since then. All areas in the city are classified as lowrisk for COVID starting Tuesday.
