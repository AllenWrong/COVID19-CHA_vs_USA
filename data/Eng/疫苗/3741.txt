Chinas COVID vaccines safe, effective expert
2020-12-21 22:43
A staff member displays samples of the COVID inactivated vaccine at Sinovac Biotech Ltd, in Beijing, capital of China, March , . PhotoXinhua    BEIJING  Clinical trials and emergency inoculations so far have shown that Chinas COVID vaccines are safe and effective, a Chinese expert said on Monday. China has already conducted nearly  million emergency inoculations, said Zheng Zhongwei, an official with the National Health Commission NHC and head of a national working group for vaccine development. He noted that such vaccinations were given on a voluntary, informed basis and in accordance with relevant laws and regulations. Some minor adverse reactions have been reported, but no serious side effects have occurred, said Zheng. Over , recipients of Chinas vaccines have traveled overseas to highrisk areas, and no serious infections have been reported, said Zheng.
