Beijing to complete COVID inoculation for key groups before Spring Festival
2021-01-04 19:38
PhotoVCG    BEIJING  Beijing will complete COVID inoculation for key groups before the Spring Festival, which falls on Feb  this year, according to local authorities. After this round of inoculation, all the eligible people in Beijing will receive COVID vaccines as the vaccines receive market approval, along with a gradual increase in vaccine production, the municipal health commission said. A total of , people received the first dose of COVID vaccine at  inoculation venues in two days since the city began administering the jab on Jan  among specific groups of people with high infection risks. Im glad to receive the vaccine for Im in charge of coldchain food transportation. I feel much safer now, said a vaccinee surnamed Tong at a temporary vaccination site in Beijings Chaoyang district.
