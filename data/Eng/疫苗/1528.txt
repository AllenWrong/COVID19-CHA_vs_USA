Vaccines vital global need, not charitable giving
2021-06-25 00:00
As the global fight against the COVID pandemic drags on, equitable and mass vaccination is the only emergency lever that countries can pull to put the brakes on the spread of the novel coronavirus and so save lives and get their economies back on their feet.Yet the way vaccines are being distributed around the world is not only unequal and very unfair, as United Nations SecretaryGeneral Antonio Guterres has said, but also a failure to make the best use of them to stop the train wreck of the pandemic from piling up.
