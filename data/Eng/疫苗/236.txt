China can contain COVID resurgence within a month Top epidemiologist
2021-10-30 15:16
Chinas top epidemiologist Zhong Nanshan. File photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn    GUANGZHOU  Chinas top epidemiologist Zhong Nanshan said that the country will be able to effectively contain the latest COVID resurgence within a month. Zhong made the remarks during a webinar attended by medical professionals on Saturday, as sporadic local COVID cases have been reported in various parts of the country. The coronavirus and the pandemic cannot be eradicated within a short period of time, said Zhong, calling on other countries to adopt strict and prioritized measures against COVID and actively roll out vaccination. Zhong said he expects that more than  percent of Chinese residents will have been fully vaccinated by the end of this year.
