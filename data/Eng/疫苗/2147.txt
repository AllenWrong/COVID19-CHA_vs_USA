Taiwan residents on the mainland are registering for vaccination
2021-04-28 20:29
Taiwan residents living on the Chinese mainland are actively registering for vaccination as the mainland recently began to offer them the same COVID vaccine access as their mainland counterparts, Ma Xiaoguang, a spokesman for the Taiwan Affairs Office of the State Council, said at a news conference on Wednesday.As of Sunday, , Taiwan residents in East Chinas Fujian province were vaccinated and as of Monday, , Taiwan residents in East Chinas Jiangsu province were vaccinated, according to Ma.As of Tuesday, , doses of the vaccine had been administered to Taiwan residents in Beijing, , in East Chinas Zhejiang province, and , in Shanghai, he said.With the expansion of the vaccination scope, more Taiwan compatriots on the mainland will sign up for vaccination, Ma said.
