China plans to approve first foreign COVID vaccine Media
2021-04-20 14:33
NEW YORK  China is planning to approve its first foreign COVID vaccine before July, according to people privy to the matter, US media reported.The clinicaltrial data of the vaccine made by Germanys BioNTech SE has been under scrutiny, The Wall Street Journal reported on Friday, adding that the domestic distribution of the shot is expected to be given green light within the the next ten weeks.BioNTech has agreed to cooperate with Shanghai Fosun Pharma to deliver  million doses to China in , pending approval, the report said.
