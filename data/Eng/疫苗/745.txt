Hurricane Ida pummels Louisiana, knocks out power in New Orleans
2021-08-31 00:00
NEW ORLEANS, LouisianaHurricane Ida battered the southern US state of Louisiana and plunged New Orleans city into darkness on Sunday, leaving at least one person dead  years to the day after Hurricane Katrina devastated the city.Ida slammed into the Louisiana coast as a Category  storm, but downgraded on Monday to a tropical storm,  hours after striking in Louisiana as a Category  hurricane, forecasters say.The storm knocked out power for all of New Orleans, leaving more than  million people across Louisiana without power, according to online tracker PowerOutage.
