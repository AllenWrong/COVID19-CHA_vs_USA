First batch of Fosun Pharmaprovided vaccines expected in Taipei on Thursday
2021-09-01 16:14
SHANGHAI  The first batch of COVID mRNA vaccines provided by the Shanghaibased Fosun Pharma to Taiwan is expected to arrive in Taipei on Thursday morning.A total of , doses of the vaccines have been dispatched from Luxembourg Airport on Wednesday afternoon Beijing time, said the company, adding that the second batch of , doses will start delivery in a week.The COVID mRNA vaccine developed by Fosun Pharma and BioNTech received authorization for emergency use and special import in Hong Kong and Macao earlier this year. The vaccine is included under the vaccination programs of the Hong Kong and Macao special administrative regions governments.
