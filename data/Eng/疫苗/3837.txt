2020-12-10 10:42

The Ministry of Health and Prevention said in a statement that the announcement is a significant vote of confidence by the UAEs health authorities in the safety and efficacy of this vaccine. The interim analysis of the phase III trials shows the Chinese vaccine provides  percent efficacy against COVID infection, according to the statement. The analysis also shows the vaccine to have  percent seroconversion rate of neutralizing antibody and  percent effectiveness in preventing moderate and severe cases of the disease. Furthermore, the analysis shows no serious safety concerns. The phase III trials in the UAE have included , volunteers from  countries and regions. The vaccine was granted Emergency Use Authorization in September by the health ministry to protect frontline workers most at risk of COVID.
