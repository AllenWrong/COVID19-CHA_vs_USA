President resolute on global community of health
2021-05-25 00:00
President Xi Jinping has pledged more action on building a global community of health for all, as officials and experts hailed Chinas response to the COVID pandemic as exemplary. Xi unveiled Chinas next steps in contributing to the global fight against COVID, including a  billion aid package for developing nations and proposals to bolster global equal access to vaccines, at the Global Health Summit on Friday.
