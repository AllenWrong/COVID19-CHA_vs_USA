Study in Brazil indicates Sinovac vaccine works against P variant found in Brazil
2021-03-13 16:45
Preliminary data from a study in Brazil indicates that the COVID vaccine developed by Chinas Sinovac Biotech Ltd is effective against the P variant of the virus first discovered in Brazil, a source familiar with the study told Reuters on Monday. The source, who did not provide data details, said the study had tested the blood of vaccinated people against the Brazilian variant of the virus. Coronavac, as the Sinovac shot is known, is the main vaccine currently being used to inoculate people in Brazil.
