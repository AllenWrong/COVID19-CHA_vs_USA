More COVID vaccines possible for Central, East Europe
2021-02-09 16:54
China stands ready to actively consider the demand for COVID vaccines from other Central and Eastern European Countries on top of its ongoing cooperation with Serbia and Hungary, President Xi Jinping said on Tuesday.In a keynote speech delivered via video link at the ChinaCEEC summit, the president noted that Serbia has already obtained  million doses of vaccines from Chinese companies and Hungary is also in cooperation with a Chinese vaccine maker.
