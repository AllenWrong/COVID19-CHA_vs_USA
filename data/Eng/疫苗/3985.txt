2020-11-21 00:00
Despite progress on jabs, public health measures remain vital, forum hears                                             Vaccines could be a game changer in the battle against the COVID pandemic but they are not a silver bullet, participants in a forum said on Thursday in stressing the need for basic safeguards such as face masks.
