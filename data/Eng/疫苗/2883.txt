China to strengthen legal safeguards for public health
2021-03-08 15:43
Students get inoculated with a COVID vaccine at a temporary site at Tsinghua University in Beijing, on March . ZOU HONGCHINA DAILY    BEIJING  Chinas top legislature will continue with the work plan for enacting and revising laws to strengthen the legal safeguards for public health, according to a work report. The annual work report of the National Peoples Congress NPC Standing Committee was submitted Monday to the ongoing fourth session of the th NPC for deliberation. The NPC Standing Committee will formulate a law on responding to public health emergencies and revise the Wildlife Protection Law, the Law on the Prevention and Control of Infectious Diseases, the Frontier Health and Quarantine Law, and the Practicing Physicians Law, said the report.
