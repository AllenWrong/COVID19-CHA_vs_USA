Russia to begin mass COVID vaccinations next week
2020-12-03 03:31
PhotoAgencies    MOSCOW  Russian President Vladimir Putin on Wednesday instructed the government to begin largescale vaccinations against the coronavirus next week. Medical workers and teachers, who are two highrisk groups, will be among the first to get shots, Putin said at an online meeting with Deputy Prime Minister Tatyana Golikova and other senior officials. According to the president, the number of produced vaccine doses will reach two million in the coming days. I am sure that we will complete all the preparations this week, Golikova reported to Putin, adding that the vaccination will be voluntary and free of charge.
