Vitality injected back into Cambodia capital
2021-11-19 00:00
Phnom Penhs bustling streets signal return to normal with Chinese jabs                                            PHNOM PENHTraffic jams rarely stir fond memories among city dwellers, but for the residents of Phnom Penh, the return of honking cars and tuktuks to the streets of the Cambodian capital signals vitality and hope after the gloom of the pandemic.For months, residents had been struggling under the weight of strict restrictions on social and business activities, especially after COVID infections rose earlier this year.A turnaround in peoples fortunes came after Chinas timely and steady supplies of vaccines against the coronavirus. And, with high vaccination rates, Cambodia has been opening up to economic and social activities.
