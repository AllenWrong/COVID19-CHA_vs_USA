Ukraine starts distributing CoronaVac vaccine to regions
2021-04-13 09:55
Staffers unload CoronaVac vaccine produced by Chinese company Sinovac Biotech at Kiev Boryspil airport in Kiev, Ukraine, March , . PhotoXinhua    KIEV  Ukraine has completed laboratory studies of the CoronaVac vaccine produced by Chinese company Sinovac Biotech and is starting to send the vaccine across the country, Health Minister Maksym Stepanov said on his official Facebook page on Monday. According to the official, CoronaVac vaccine will be provided to police officers, people with limited mobility and their guardians, Olympians and Paralympians. Ukraine officially registered the CoronaVac vaccine for use against COVID on March . The first batch of CoronaVac vaccine arrived in Ukraine on March . Ukraines Lekhim Group company, which has a contract with Sinovac Biotech on the distribution and localization of production in Ukraine, is to deliver the following batches of the COVID vaccine to Ukraine under a contract with the Ministry of Health for the supply of . million doses of vaccine. COVID numbers have been growing in Ukraine over the past month against the backdrop of the vaccination campaign, which started on Feb. .
