COVID vaccines enter clinical trials in China health official
2021-07-08 19:36
BEIJING  Twentytwo COVID vaccines have been approved to enter clinical trials in China, a health official said Thursday.To date, four COVID vaccines have been granted conditional marketing approval and three have been authorized for emergency use within the country, said Yuan Lin, an official with the National Medical Products Administration NMPA, at a press conference in Beijing.By the end of June, the NMPA had granted emergency approval for clinical trials to  drugs intended for the treatment of COVID and its indications, Yuan added.
