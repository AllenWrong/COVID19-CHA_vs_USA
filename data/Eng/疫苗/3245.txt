Colombia announces emergency use of Sinovac vaccines
2021-02-04 14:13
Colombias President Ivan Duque PhotoAgencies    BOGOTA  Colombian President Ivan Duque announced on Wednesday that the countrys National Food and Drug Surveillance Institute has authorized the emergency use of COVID vaccines developed by Chinese firm Sinovac Biotech. The approval was made following experts evaluation of the vaccines, Duque said during a daily television program on COVID. According to the Colombian government, the country will launch a mass vaccination campaign against the COVID pandemic on Feb. . Colombia has registered ,, COVID infections and , deaths as of Wednesday, said the Ministry of Health and Social Protection. The government has extended the health emergency over COVID till Feb. , urging people to avoid crowds and observe safety measures to curb the spread of the virus.
