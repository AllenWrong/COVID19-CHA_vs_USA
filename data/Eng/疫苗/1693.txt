Chinese nasal spray, inhaled COVID vaccines undergoing clinical trial Official
2021-06-07 11:54
BEIJING  Clinical trials of Chinadeveloped nasal spray and inhaled COVID vaccines are currently underway, according to the National Health Commission.The nasal spray has been selected as one of Chinas vaccine technologies to combat COVID in the very beginning, Zeng Yixin, deputy head of the commission, said in a recent interview.Medical experts will study the safety and effectiveness of the tested vaccines after completing the collection of clinical trial data, Zeng added.
