Working group sent to guide COVID control in Liaoning
2021-05-16 13:49
People receive COVID vaccines at a vaccination site in Dadong district of Shenyang, Northeast Chinas Liaoning province, May , .PhotoXinhua    BEIJING  The National Health Commission NHC has dispatched a working group to guide the COVID prevention and control efforts in Northeast Chinas Liaoning province, the NHC said on Sunday. Liaoning reported four confirmed COVID cases between  am and  pm on Saturday, with one reported in the provincial capital Shenyang and three others in the city of Yingkou, said the provincial health commission. A total of five asymptomatic carriers were also reported in Yingkou on Saturday, the local health commission added. As of Saturday, nine residential communities and villages in Yingkou had been upgraded to mediumrisk areas for COVID.
