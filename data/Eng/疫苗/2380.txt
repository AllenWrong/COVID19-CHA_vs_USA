Wuhanon the rise a year after lockdown was lifted
2021-04-08 00:00
One year after a lockdown was lifted, Wuhan, capital of Hubei province, the city hardest hit by COVID on the Chinese mainland, has recovered from the outbreak and is developing at an even more rapid pace than before, experts and local residents said.On April  last year, the city reopened after a day lockdown brought on by the COVID outbreak. It has reported no local COVID cases for more than  months, since May .Mass vaccination of Wuhan residents was launched in December to further strengthen the safety and health of the people.
