Position Paper of the Peoples Republic of China On the th Anniversary of the United Nations
2020-09-11 00:00
I. The year  marks the th anniversary of the founding of the United Nations UN and the th anniversary of the end of the World AntiFascist War and the Chinese Peoples War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression. Seventyfive years ago, with the determination to save succeeding generations from the scourge of war, which twice in our lifetime has brought untold sorrow to mankind, we the peoples founded the UN as the most universal, representative and authoritative international organization and established an international order and system underpinned by the purposes and principles of the UN Charter. Thus began a new era in mankinds pursuit of peace and development. Over the past  years, with peace as its mission, the UN has set up and operated a collective security mechanism, engaged in active mediation to settle disputes peacefully and deployed over  peacekeeping missions to conflict zones. In the past three quarters of a century, the UN has been instrumental in mitigating regional conflicts, preventing new world wars, and maintaining overall peace and stability in the world. Over the past  years, with development as its goal, the UN has capitalized on the trend of economic globalization and mobilized resources on a global scale to establish and implement the Millennium Development Goals and the  Agenda for Sustainable Development. With its help, billions of people are marching toward modernization. Smallpox and many other infectious diseases that plagued humanity for millennia were eradicated.
