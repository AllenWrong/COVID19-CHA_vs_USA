Philippines yearns for tourism comeback
2021-11-24 00:00
MANILAAfter more than a year in the doldrums, tourism in the coastal Philippine town of Mabini is enjoying a rebound.Known as the birthplace of scuba diving in the Philippines, tourists are returning to see the towns beautiful seaside scenery and explore the rich marine life underwater.Thankfully, the pandemic is being handled well, and the cases are in a downward trend.
