Strong willingness shown to get COVID inoculation
2020-12-31 00:00
People qualified to get an emergency inoculation against COVID in China have shown a strong willingness to take the shot, as evidence of homegrown vaccines safety and efficacy continues to build, boosting the publics confidence. Leading vaccine developer Sinopharm said on Wednesday that an inactivated vaccine candidate created by a company unit in Beijing is . percent effective in protecting people against the virus and is generally safe, citing interim results from stage  clinical trials.
