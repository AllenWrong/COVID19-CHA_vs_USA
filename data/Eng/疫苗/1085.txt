Shanghai government day quarantine for travelers from high risk areas
2021-07-29 15:52
People take COVID vaccines at the temporary vaccination site at Shanghai Hongqiao Railway Station in East Chinas Shanghai, May , . PhotoXinhua    People traveling to Shanghai from high COVID risk areas are required to report to residential communities or designated hotels within  hours upon arrival and undergo a day quarantine, the municipal government reiterated on Thursday. These individuals must be quarantined at designated facilities for  days and take two COVID tests, said the citys epidemic prevention and control office. People from middle risk areas are encouraged to stay in and avoid going out unless necessary. They must also report their health condition every day and take two COVID tests. The office also instructed public institutions such as schools and universities to adopt stricter prevention measures and urged citizens not to travel to middle or high risk areas within China or overseas.
