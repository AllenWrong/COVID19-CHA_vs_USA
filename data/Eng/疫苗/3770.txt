Putin hopes for improved ties with US
2020-12-18 00:00
Russian leader highlights expectations of relations under new Biden administration                     Russian President Vladimir Putin said on Thursday that he hopes to overcome problems in relations with the United States with the advent of the Biden administration.  We expect all problems, which emerged, or at least some of them, will be ironed out under the new administration, Putin said during his annual news conference in Moscow.  We presume that the newly elected US president will understand what is going on. He is a person who has experience in both domestic and foreign policy, he said.  The Russian president, who did not congratulate Presidentelect Joe Biden until Tuesday, after Biden received Electoral College confirmation, spoke of his expectations for relations with the US under a new administration.  Amid the restrictions due to the COVID pandemic, Putins customary yearend news conference was held online this year.  Putin answered questions from his residence in the Moscow suburb of NovoOgaryovo, and journalists from state, foreign and regional media posed their queries over video link from one of six centers across the country.
