Infections surge after holiday as US relaxes curbs
2020-06-12 00:00
New coronavirus cases and hospitalizations are spiking in several US states, including Arizona, where state health officials have urged hospitals to activate emergency measures, and in California, where officials have placed nine counties with half the states population on a watch list.Since the Memorial Day weekend of May , admissions to hospitals due to coronavirus have increased in at least seven other states North and South Carolina, Oregon, Arkansas, Mississippi, Utah and Texas, The Washington Post reported.Texas, one of the first states to loosen restrictions, reported the number of new infections has climbed  percent across the state in the past two weeks.
