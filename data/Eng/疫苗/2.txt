PhotoXinhua    KUALA LUMPUR  China will donate another  million doses of the Sinovac vaccine to Malaysia, The Malaysia Reserve reported Tuesday. Malaysian Foreign Minister Saifuddin Abdullah was quoted as saying that China has so far donated  million doses of the Sinovac vaccine to ASEAN the Association of Southeast Asian Nations countries, among which , were for Malaysia. On top of additional vaccines, China will also help ASEAN bolster its public health and pharmaceutical capabilities through the sharing of technology, expertise and intellectual property, the report said. This will help ASEAN strengthen its public health system and increase preparedness to address future health emergencies, Abdullah told a press conference after the virtual ASEANChina Special Summit on Monday.
