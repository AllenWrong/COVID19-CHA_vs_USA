Presidents speech shows China is a responsible global player
2021-09-13 00:00
Xis address to th BRICS Summit a blueprint for bloc, top diplomat says                    President Xi Jinpings speech at the th BRICS Summit showed China is honoring its responsibilities as a major global player by promoting international cooperation to fight the COVID pandemic and taking practical measures to ensure accessible and affordable vaccines, a senior Chinese diplomat said.ViceForeign Minister Ma Zhaoxu made the remark at a news briefing on Thursday after Xi addressed the summit via video link in Beijing. In order to enhance global vaccine cooperation and realize the equitable access of vaccines for all, Xi announced, China will donate an additional  million COVID vaccine doses to developing countries by the end of the year to contribute further to global cooperation to battle the pandemic.At a crucial time when the world is faced with the pandemic and challenges not seen in a century, Ma said that Xis participation in the meeting helped draw a blueprint for BRICS cooperation and gave directions for global efforts to fight the virus and promote global economic recovery. BRICS is a bloc that consists of five developing countries Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa.As the pandemic accelerated changes to the international landscape, some countries have played small clique politics and engaged in unilateralism disguised as multilateralism, Ma said.However, Xis call for true multilateralism, which includes adhering to the purposes and principles of the United Nations Charter and safeguarding the UNcentered international system, added more stability in a world full of uncertainties.In the New Delhi Declaration adopted by the summit, leaders also reiterated their belief that effective and representative multilateralism is essential for enhancing resilience against current and future global challenges, promoting peoples wellbeing and building a sustainable future for the planet.Speaking of Xis approach of promoting common development, Ma said it not only gave directions for the world to realize stronger, greener and healthier development after the pandemic, but also boosted the confidence of all countries, especially emerging economies and developing nations, to implement the  Agenda for Sustainable Development.The viceminister also said that as the bellwether for emerging market countries, BRICS plays a vital role in facilitating global recovery and narrowing the development divide.Xis proposals of strengthening unity and further enhancing cooperation among BRICS members will help ensure the longterm and stable development of cooperation within the bloc, Ma added.In his speech, Xi said that in the current circumstances, it is important for BRICS countries to keep their resolve, strengthen unity and further enhance the quality of practical cooperation.He proposed strengthening BRICS cooperation in the areas of the economy, policy, security and peopletopeople exchanges.China will work with other BRICS countries to implement all the proposals reached during the summit, Ma
