Chinas st selfdeveloped COVID vaccine gets marketing goahead
2020-12-31 21:56
China on Thursday granted conditional marketing authorization for its first selfdeveloped COVID vaccine, which will be provided for all residents free of charge.In Shenzhen, nine highrisk groups, including people who want to go abroad for study and business purposes, were allowed to get emergency COVID vaccination for free a few days ago.
