Chinas Red Cross makes vaccine donation to Bangladesh
2021-10-27 17:37
, domestically produced COVID vaccines donated to Bangladesh leave Beijing on Monday. Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn    The Red Cross Society of China said on Wednesday it has donated , domestically produced COVID vaccines to the Bangladesh Red Crescent Society. These doses, made by domestic drugmaker Sinopharm, arrived in Bangladesh on Tuesday. The donation is expected to aid Bangladeshs fight against the novel coronavirus and protect the health of its people.
