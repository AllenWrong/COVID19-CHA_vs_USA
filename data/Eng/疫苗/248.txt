Shanghai to begin vaccinating booster doses to citizens
2021-10-29 16:00
A medical worker administers a shot of the COVID vaccine to a resident at a community health center in Xuhui district of Shanghai, Jan , . PhotoXinhua    SHANGHAI  Shanghai will begin vaccination of COVID vaccine boosters for free to its citizens starting from Nov , local authorities announced on Friday. Citizens aged  or above need to be accompanied by their family members or volunteers during their vaccination, said the municipalitys COVID prevention and control leading group office. Residents from Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan, overseas Chinese and foreign nationals will be able to receive vaccine boosters later according to further arrangements.
