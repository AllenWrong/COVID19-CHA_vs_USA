Sri Lankas vaccination rate speeds up
2021-08-03 00:00
Country hoping all citizens age  and over will be given shots by September                     With the timely and sufficient supply of donated and purchased COVID vaccines from China, Sri Lanka has become a country with one of the highest vaccination rates.  As more Sinopharm vaccines are set to arrive this week, the country hopes to ensure all its citizens aged  or above will be inoculated by the end of August, according to the nations Presidential Secretariat.
