British Health Secretary Javid tests positive for COVID
2021-07-17 23:35
British Health Secretary Sajid Javid has tested positive for COVID, and is selfisolating at home with his family while waiting for the result of a PCR test.Javid, who became health secretary in June, revealed on Twitter that he tested positive after a lateral flow test on Friday night, with very mild symptoms, and was thankful that he has had both his jabs of the COVID vaccine.He urged people who have not yet been vaccinated to come forward for their jabs.The news came as the United Kingdom registered more than , daily cases, for the first time since midJanuary.Most of Englands pandemic restrictions are due to be lifted on Monday, despite experts warning that this unlocking could be a threat to global health.
