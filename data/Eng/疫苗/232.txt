Xi calls for enhancing vaccine accessibility, affordability in developing countries
2021-10-30 20:39
A handover ceremony for Chinesedonated COVID vaccines is held in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, on Oct , . PhotoXinhua    BEIJING  China is ready to work with all parties to enhance vaccine accessibility and affordability in developing countries and make positive contribution to building a global line of defense through vaccination, President Xi Jinping said on Saturday. Xi made the remarks while addressing the th Group of  Leaders Summit via video link in Beijing. China, together with  other countries, has also launched an Initiative for Belt and Road Partnership on COVID Vaccines Cooperation, Xi noted.
