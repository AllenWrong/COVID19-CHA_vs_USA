PhotoAgencies    BEIJING  An opinion poll by Ipsos for the World Economic Forum shows about three quarters of the respondents in the world would like to get a COVID vaccine if it becomes available. The survey included responses from nearly , people from  countries, with the most enthusiasm in China and the least in Russia. Experts are concerned that more than a quarter of people worldwide would not get a vaccine. The  shortfall in vaccine confidence is significant enough to compromise the effectiveness of rolling out a COVID vaccine, said Arnaud Bernaert, head of Shaping the Future of Health and Healthcare at the World Economic Forum, in a statement.
