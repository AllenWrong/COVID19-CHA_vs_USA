Euro  to take place in  cities  report
2021-03-17 10:01
The UEFA Euro  trophy is displayed marking  days before the start of the Euro  soccer tournament in St. Petersburg, Russia, March , . PhotoAgencies    BERLIN  It has been reported that Euro  will still take place in  cities across the continent despite the Covid pandemic. A member of European football governing body UEFA executive committee said the goal was to stick with the city plan. The goal remains to be able to hold onto the mode of the event, Rainer Koch, a vice president in the German federation DFB was quoted as saying by dpa. He admitted that it was too early for final statements but showed confidence in the vaccines whose effect will be much stronger in a couple of weeks. Euro  is scheduled to take place between June  and July ,  after it was pushed back by one year.
