WHO announces landmark intl collaboration to speed up COVID vaccines development
2020-04-24 21:35
GENEVA  The World Health Organization WHO on Friday launched a landmark international collaboration to accelerate the development of COVID health technologies. The new collaboration was launched at a virtual event cohosted by WHO DirectorGeneral Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus, French President Emmanuel Macron, and European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen. Addressing the opening of the event, Tedros said that our shared commitment is to ensure all people have access to all the tools to defeat COVID. Were coordinating a global trial on the safety and efficacy of four therapeutics against COVID, the WHO chief said.
