XinhuaCao Yiming    BEIJING  The Chinese mainland on Tuesday reported eight new locally transmitted COVID cases, the National Health Commission said in its daily report on Wednesday. All of the new local cases were reported in Liaoning, said the commission. Also reported were  new imported cases, according to the commission. Two new suspected cases arriving from outside the mainland were reported in Shanghai on Tuesday, said the commission. No new deaths related to COVID were reported on Tuesday, it added.
