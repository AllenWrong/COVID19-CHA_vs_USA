Chinas Ruili clears areas with medium, high COVID risks
2021-05-04 22:42
A woman measures blood pressure at a COVID vaccination site of Jingcheng Hospital in Ruili city, Southwest Chinas Yunnan province, April , . PhotoXinhua    KUNMING  Ruili, a border city in Southwest Chinas Yunnan province, now has no medium or highrisk areas for the novel coronavirus, the citys COVID prevention and control headquarters announced Tuesday. COVID risk levels in two areas of the city were downgraded from medium to low from  pm Tuesday, marking the clearance of all areas with medium or high infection risks in Ruili. Dozens of domesticallytransmitted COVID cases have been reported in Ruili since March . Local authorities launched several citywide COVID testing campaigns to curb the spread of the virus.
