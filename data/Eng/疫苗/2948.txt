Biden says US on track to enough vaccines for every adult in US by endMay
2021-03-03 05:46
US President Joe Biden. PhotoAgencies    WASHINGTON  President Joe Biden on Tuesday said the United States was on track to have enough vaccines for every adult in the country by the end of May. Biden announced that Merck  Co Inc will help make rival Johnson  Johnsons singleshot COVID vaccine in a partnership that he said was similar to those seen during World War Two. With three vaccines now available, Biden said he was confident the country would reach his goal of delivering  million COVID vaccine doses in  days. Reuters
