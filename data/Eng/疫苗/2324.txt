Variant can break through Pfizer shot
2021-04-13 00:00
Researchers in Israel say the novel coronavirus variant first discovered in South Africa is able to break through PfizerBioNTechs vaccine to some extent. The variant managed to avoid protection offered by two doses of the vaccine to some degree, according to a firstofitskind realworld data study by Tel Aviv University and Israels largest healthcare provider, Clalit.
