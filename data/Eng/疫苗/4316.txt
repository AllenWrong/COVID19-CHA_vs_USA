Chinadeveloped nasal spray vaccine for COVID approved for clinical trials
2020-09-09 22:17
HONG KONG  The University of Hong Kong HKU Wednesday announced that the clinical trials of a nasal spray vaccine for COVID jointly developed by the universitys Department of Microbiology and mainland institutions have been approved.The State Key Laboratory of Emerging Infectious Diseases has been working on the vaccine with Xiamen University and Beijing Wantai Biological Pharmacy.It is the first nasal spray COVID vaccine approved by the Chinas National Medical Products Administration for clinical trial in humans.The HKU said in a statement that its vaccine strategy has been selected as one of the five vaccine technologies by the Ministry of Science and Technology for further evaluation.
