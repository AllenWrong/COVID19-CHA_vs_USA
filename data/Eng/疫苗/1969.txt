Anhui steps up COVID prevention
2021-05-15 00:00
Central and provincial health authorities are taking protective measures like tracking more close contacts of infected patients and preventing further transmissions after East Chinas Anhui and Northeast Chinas Liaoning provinces reported six locally transmitted COVID cases from Thursday to Friday.As of Friday evening, Anhui and Liaoning had four and two confirmed cases, in addition to one and three asymptomatic cases, respectively, all locally transmitted, according to press conferences by the local health authorities. So far, Anhuis Hefei and Luan cities both reported two confirmed cases, according to the local health commissions.A woman surnamed Zhang, who works in a photography studio in Luan, tested positive for the novel coronavirus early on Thursday morning at a hospital.
